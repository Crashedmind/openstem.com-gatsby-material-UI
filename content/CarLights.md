# Car Lights and Horn
Horn sounds when button pressed.

Lights indicate Movement


 Movement      | Button        | Lights  
 ------------- |:-------------:| -----:
 Forward       | Green         | green 
 Backward      | Yellow        | yellow 
 Stop          | Red           | red 
 Left          | other      | left yellow lights flash 
 Right         | other      | right yellow lights flash
