# Car Lights and Horn
Horn sounds when button pressed.

Lights indicate Movement


| Movement      | Trigger        |     |
|:------------- |:-------------| :-----|
| Stop          | Red            | red | 
| Left          | 2 way switch  | left yellow lights flash front and rear |
| Right         | 2 way switch  | right yellow lights flash front and rear |
| -             | Blue button    | Horn beeps |
